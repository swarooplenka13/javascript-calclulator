LUCKY NUMBER:

U are given a integer  n, you have to find out all positive integers m such that (n mod m)<=n/2.

CONSTRAINTS: 1<=n<=1000000

INPUT: FIRST LINE CONSISTS OF TESTCASES
       Second line consists of single integer n.
       testcases<=10.

OUTPUT: output a single integer of count of all positive integers m .

SAMPLE INPUT:
1
5

SAMPLE OUTPUT:
5
